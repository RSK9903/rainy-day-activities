Our Site

We are Team 8, a project division in CS 146. We have put together this page in the hopes to help those that suffer from severe boredom. Our goal is to create a hub of any and all activities that can be performed in doors. We are always open to suggestions. There is a suggestion box located at the very bottom of the home page. Please feel free to leave us comments and suggestions regarding our content or anything you feel we should add. Thank you!
