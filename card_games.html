Games that need a standard deck of cards

If you don't have a deck of cards you can purchase one here!

  • BS
    Object of the Game: Get rid of all your cards before anyone else.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Go Fish
    Object of the Game: Win the most "books" of cards. A book is any four of a kind, such as four kings, four aces, and so on.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Old Maid
    Object of the Game: Form and discard pairs of cards, and not to be left with the odd card (a queen) at the end.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Rummy
    Object of the Game: Each player tries to form matched sets consisting of groups of three or four of a kind, or sequences of three or more cards of the same suit.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Solitaire
    Object of the Game: Each player tries to form matched sets consisting of groups of three or four of a kind, or sequences of three or more cards of the same suit.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Spades
    Object of the Game: To win at least the number of tricks bid.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • Spit
    Object of the Game: The winner is the first player to get rid of all his cards.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here

  • War
    Object of the Game: TThe goal is to be the first player to win all 52 cards.

    Read in-depth rules here or watch a how-to YouTube video here
